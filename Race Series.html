Race Series

-Intercontinental GT Challenge: This global championship takes in the world’s most iconic racetracks across five continents – Bathurst in Australia, Spa in Europe, Suzuka in Asia, Indianapolis in the USA and Kyalami in Africa. Bentley Team M-Sport will enter the full championship with sights set on the overall win.

-GT World Challenge Europe: Formerly the Blancpain GT Series, this is the most established international GT3 championship in the world. Featuring the top tracks in Europe, the ten events are split into endurance races and sprint races. In the Endurance Cup, K-PAX Racing will race for the Pro class honours and Team Parker Racing will contest the Silver Cup class for Bentley Motorsport Academy.

-Le Mans Cup: Le Mans holds a dear place in the heart of any Bentley fan. Afterall, Bentley has taken the world’s most coveted race win four times. The Le Mans Cup was established to provide a ladder to the 24 Hours of Le Mans – where teams can compete on Europe’s top racetracks including the famous French circuit. In 2020, new customer team Bullitt Racing will enter the full season with its new Continental GT3.

-British GT Championship: The British national GT championship takes in tracks including Silverstone, Brands Hatch and Bentley’s home circuit Oulton Park. The 2017 championship-winning outfit Team Parker Racing is back in 2020 and its GT4 championship-winning drivers have stepped-up to GT3 to battle for overall honours.
